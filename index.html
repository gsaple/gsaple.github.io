Welcome
Welcome to Surrey Hills Day Care Centre, where little hearts bloom in a nurturing, joyful, and loving environment.

Surrey Hills Day Care Centre is a 74 place centre in beautiful Surrey Hills. Proudly owned by two local Melbourne families, Surrey Hills Day Care Centre prides itself on creating a home away from home for our children, families and community.

Conveniently located next to Chatham station, we provide high quality care and education for children from 6 weeks to school age, including 4 year old kindergarten.

At the core of our approach to learning and care is the belief that all children ought to feel empowered, secure, nurtured and loved. We believe all children deserve choices, autonomy, consistency and access to education. Children are equally valued, viewed as independent learners and their achievements and learning are celebrated.
